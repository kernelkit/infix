Infix NOS Validation
====================

In the world of network operating systems, an untested feature is worse
than no feature at all.  Which is why Infix relies on Infamy —
our comprehensive validation framework that ensures every change meets
our exacting standards before it reaches your network.  Named with a
hint of humor (because let's face it, failing tests should be infamous),
this system guards the gates between development and deployment,
catching issues before they have a chance to make history.

Infamy runs your code through the gauntlet at both pre-commit and CI/CD
stages, focusing on functional testing to ensure that what worked for
you yesterday still works today.  Because in networking, surprises are
rarely welcome.

Our software validation is vital in ensuring we deliver the best and most
reliable software to our customers.  This commitment to quality is attracting
attention from companies and organizations worldwide, who recognize the
value of a robust network infrastructure.

By integrating tests directly into Infix's source code, we are pioneering
a new approach to software quality assurance for industrial network
solutions. This tight coupling between code and validation ensures that
reliability isn't just an afterthought — it's built into our DNA.
